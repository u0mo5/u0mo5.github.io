程序员必须知道的编程格言

1.保持简单直白(Keep It Simple Stupid) 2.不要自我复制(Don’t Repeat Yourself) 3.能干的人解决问题。智慧的人绕开问题(A clever person solves a problem. A wise person avoids it)– Einstein 4.沉默会被理解为赞同(Silence is construed as approval)( Picked from Kevin blog ) 5.没有火就不会有烟(There is no smoke without fire) 6.先想好，后编程(Think first, Program later) 7.永远不要假设计算机为你假设了任何前提(Never assume the computer assumes anything)
